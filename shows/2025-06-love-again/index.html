THE LOVE AGAIN SET

JUNE 2025 — AUSTIN, TX

I loaned out my gear but missed my slot. Not a main one - easily replaced by a playlist. Or so they thought.

Sunday. 8pm. Party's winding down. People leaving. Sound system is hot. I have my portable decks and hop on to the system. Renegade-style.

Three hours later no one has left because the music is that good. This is the moment - after years in Brooklyn and a 2024 lost to mold toxicity - we realize just what I can do behind decks.

It all started here.
